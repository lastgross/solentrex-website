Solentrex AI Roof Dynamics

Solentrex Roof Dynamics is an advanced AI system that uses Hi-Res Imaging, DSM data, Lidar and Perez modeling to calculate roof dimensions, shading, segmentation, azimuth, roof impediments, and existing solar systems.

Solentrex AI Panel Optimization

Utilizing the Solentrex AI Utility, the Solentrex Panel Optimization system analyzes the energy output requirements along with the desired target offset and optimizes panel placement both balancing maximum panel output, shading, and aesthetics.

Accuracy

Industry-Leading Production and Shading Numbers

Solentrex delivers 1.13% production variance from actual real-world production data — while most proposal tools see a variance over 10%. This was achieved using Advanced AI Shading and every panel is calculated individually based on its specific azimuth, tilt, and irradiance using NREL weather data, the Perez model, LIDAR, and soiling calculated down to zip code level. Multiple DSM data options ensure coverage in rural areas.

AI-Powered

Automation That Actually Works

Our AI reads utility bills with 100% accuracy — over 10,000 development hours went into this alone. It automatically populates usage data, costs, and account numbers. Auto utility and tariff detection ensures the right rate schedule from day one. Automatic title checks return 83 data points about property owners, preventing errors and change orders before they happen. AI-driven segmentation and setback compliance are applied automatically during design. Connected directly to USPS via Smarty for precise rooftop coordinates.
