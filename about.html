About

Built Because the Industry
Deserved Better

Solentrex was born from frustration — a COO who lived through the chaos of fragmented solar tools and knew it could all be done in one system.

Origin

The Problem We Saw

Our COO had a direct, hands-on experience in solar operations — and it was painful. Design tools that didn't talk to CRMs. Proposals that took 30 minutes. E-signatures that cost per use. Finance portals in separate tabs. Change orders caused by averaged production numbers. Teams stitching together 5+ tools just to close a single deal.

The vision was clear: build one platform that does everything, and make it the most accurate tool in the industry. Not another integration layer — a complete replacement.
